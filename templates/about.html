What is LocalShopee?


LocalShopee is a platform which connects shopkeepers and Customers. Our mission is to provide a platform to all the shopkeepers who wants to sell their goods as well as make it easy for people like hostelites, employees, workers to find goods at one stop! The cutomers can order goods from the shop of their choice! Anyone willing to sell their products can sign up to our website and can sell the products of their choice. So, in this way, we promote and help the business of local shops to grow and expand.
